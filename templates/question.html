Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I have a persistent headache?

If you have a persistent headache, try to rest and stay hydrated. Avoid stress and bright lights. If the headache continues, please consult a healthcare professional.

How can I reduce fever at home?

To reduce fever, stay hydrated and rest. You can also use over-the-counter fever reducers like acetaminophen. If your fever is very high or persistent, seek medical advice.

When should I consult a doctor?

Consult a doctor if you experience severe symptoms, persistent pain, high fever, or any other condition that worries you or does not improve with home care.
